# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small business owners — owner-operators who run their own sales pipeline, follow-up sequences, customer communications, and marketing without a dedicated ops team. They use Stone AIO throughout the workday: checking the pipeline in the morning, following up on contacts, monitoring conversations across email and SMS, reviewing campaign performance in the evening. Their time is limited and attention is expensive. Every screen interaction must feel worth it.

## Product Purpose

Stone AIO is an all-in-one CRM and business operations platform for small businesses. It replaces a fragmented stack of separate CRM, email marketing, SMS, and automation tools with a single coherent system. Success looks like: the owner logs in, immediately sees what needs attention, acts on it, and moves on — without consulting a manual or feeling overwhelmed.

## Brand Personality

Premium, sharp, reliable. Stone AIO feels like a high-end professional tool — not a startup toy or enterprise bloatware. The interface has weight, depth, and clarity. It is quietly confident: it does not shout, it delivers.

## Anti-references

- Consumer/mobile app aesthetics: bright primary colors everywhere, over-rounded corners, playful iconography, excessive padding — the UI must feel like a business tool, not a lifestyle app
- Generic SaaS CRM look (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce): blue-dominant, cluttered, impersonal, feels like a legacy product from 2015

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity under density** — Small business owners have limited time. Every element must justify its presence. Dense layouts are acceptable when they are readable and scannable.
2. **Quiet confidence** — The interface does not need to shout. Restraint, depth, and consistent craft signal premium quality better than decoration.
3. **Immediate orientation** — Users must know where they are and what to do next within one glance. Navigation, hierarchy, and affordances must be obvious without explanation.
4. **Earned motion** — Animations and visual accents are purposeful, not decorative. They confirm actions, guide attention, and reduce cognitive load.
5. **Feels like an investment** — Every interaction should reinforce the sense that this tool was built by people who care deeply about craft.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support prefers-reduced-motion. Ensure sufficient contrast in both dark (navy) and light (warm cream) themes. Keyboard navigability for all primary workflows.
